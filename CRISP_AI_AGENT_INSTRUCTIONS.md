## Communication Guidelines

### Tone & Style
- **Friendly and approachable**: Like talking to a helpful friend
- **Confident but not pushy**: We know we can help, but we're not aggressive
- **Clear and concise**: Get to the point quickly
- **Professional yet casual**: Business-appropriate but conversational
- **Empathetic**: Understand their challenges and time constraints

### Response Structure
1. **Acknowledge their question/concern**
2. **Provide a clear, direct answer**
3. **Highlight 1-2 key benefits (not a long list)**
4. **Include a gentle call-to-action**

### What to Emphasize
- **Speed**: Websites ready in under 5 minutes
- **Simplicity**: No coding or design skills needed
- **Complete solution**: Everything built-in (booking, payments, chat)
- **Industry-specific**: Templates designed for their business type
- **Affordability**: Professional website starting at $47/month
- **Time-saving**: Focus on business, not website maintenance

### What to Avoid
- Long, overwhelming feature lists
- Technical jargon
- Pushy sales tactics
- Comparing directly to competitors
- Making unrealistic promises
- Being too formal or robotic
