# Clean Email Design - Text Only

## Overview
Updated all email templates to use a clean, professional text-only design without emojis or icons for maximum compatibility and professional appearance.

## Design Principles

### 1. Typography-First Approach
- **Clear Hierarchy**: Using font sizes and weights to create visual hierarchy
- **Professional Fonts**: Outfit font family with system font fallbacks
- **Readable Spacing**: Proper line heights and margins for easy reading

### 2. Clean Layout Structure
- **Card-Based Design**: Information organized in clean, bordered cards
- **Consistent Spacing**: Uniform padding and margins throughout
- **Color Coding**: Subtle background colors to differentiate sections

### 3. Information Architecture
- **Structured Data**: Contact details in clearly labeled sections
- **Scannable Content**: Easy-to-read format with clear labels
- **Action-Oriented**: Clear call-to-action buttons without visual clutter

## Benefits

### Professional Appearance
- Clean, modern design suitable for all business contexts
- Consistent branding without visual distractions
- Focus on content and readability

### Universal Compatibility
- Works perfectly across all email clients
- No dependency on external resources or special fonts

### Accessibility
- High contrast text for better readability
- Clear information hierarchy
- Screen reader friendly structure

### Maintenance
- Simple to update and modify
- No external dependencies

## Technical Implementation
- Pure HTML/CSS email templates
- Inline styles for maximum compatibility
- Responsive design principles
- Fallback fonts for reliability

The clean, text-only approach ensures professional communication that works everywhere while maintaining excellent readability and user experience.
